Overview

Welcome to beingAnujChaudhary — the personal portfolio of Anuj Chaudhary, accessible at beinganujchaudhary.web.app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using this Website, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Contact Form Submissions: When you use the contact form, we collect your name, email address, message content, and approximate geographic location (city, region, country, and IP address).
  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymised usage data including pages visited, session duration, device type, browser, and general geographic location. IP addresses are anonymised by Google before processing.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely for:

  • Responding to enquiries submitted via the contact form.

  • Tracking key events (e.g., form submissions) as part of our analytics reporting.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

This Website integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Collects anonymised usage statistics. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • EmailJS: Processes and delivers contact form submissions. See EmailJS Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Hosting (Google): Hosts this Website. See Firebase Privacy.
  • GeoJS: Determines the approximate location of contact form submitters. See GeoJS Privacy Policy.
4. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are stored in our email inbox and retained only as long as needed to respond to your enquiry. Analytics data is retained in Google Analytics for 14 months by default. We do not operate a standalone database for visitor records.

To exercise any of these rights, email: beinganujchaudhary@gmail.com.
